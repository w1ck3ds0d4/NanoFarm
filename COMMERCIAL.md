The placeholder iso sprites in `app/src/pixi/tiles.ts` are drawn with PixiJS `Graphics` primitives and are covered by the MIT license alongside the rest of the source. The banner SVG in `assets/banner.svg` is also MIT-licensed.

If a future phase ships sprites pulled from third-party asset packs (planned for phase 2, see [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md)), those will carry their original licenses and be documented in a `THIRD_PARTY.md` file when they land.
